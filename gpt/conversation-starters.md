# FitbodGPT - Conversation Starters

## Starters

### 1. "Paste my Fitbod report and analyze my training"
**Purpose:** Primary entry point. Gets users into the paste-first flow immediately.
**Triggers:** Full analysis pipeline - format detection, data parsing, level detection, equipment inference, injury check, summary presentation.
**Best for:** First-time users who want the full experience.

### 2. "Build me a workout plan for this week"
**Purpose:** Action-oriented users who want a plan fast.
**Triggers:** If no report pasted yet, asks for one. If report already in conversation, jumps to goal-setting, split selection, plan generation.
**Best for:** Returning users who have already had their data analyzed.

### 3. "What are my strength trends and progress?"
**Purpose:** Analytics-focused entry. Users who want insights, not necessarily a new plan.
**Triggers:** Report parsing, trend analysis, progression tracking, stalled lift detection, PR identification.
**Best for:** Users tracking progress over time, checking if they are improving.

### 4. "I have an injury - help me train around it"
**Purpose:** Injury-aware entry. Users recovering from or working around an injury.
**Triggers:** Asks for the report plus injury details, then builds a plan that avoids the affected area and suggests safe alternatives or rehab exercises.
**Best for:** Users with current injuries, pain, or limitations who still want to train safely.
