# FitbodGPT - Conversation Starters

## Starters

### 1. "Analyze my Fitbod training data"
**Purpose:** Primary entry point. The GPT will ask the user to paste their report, then run the full analysis pipeline.
**Best for:** First-time users who want the full experience.

### 2. "Build me a workout plan for this week"
**Purpose:** Action-oriented. The GPT asks for the report if not already provided, then moves to goal-setting and plan generation.
**Best for:** Users who want a plan quickly.

### 3. "What are my strength trends and progress?"
**Purpose:** Analytics-focused. The GPT asks for the report, then focuses on trend analysis, stalled lifts, and PRs.
**Best for:** Users tracking progress over time.

### 4. "I have an injury - help me train around it"
**Purpose:** Injury-aware entry. The GPT asks for the report plus injury details, then builds a plan that avoids the affected area and suggests safe alternatives.
**Best for:** Users with current injuries or limitations who still want to train safely.
